The key difference in this modpack is that you start on an island in the sky rather than in a typical Minecraft world. While it may seem similar to a standard skyblock experience, there are some notable differences that set it apart.

This modpack makes use of Ex Deorum to obtain stone blocks and string. While the early stages can feel a bit tedious due to this mechanic, progression speeds up quickly once you establish a steady resource flow.

All The Mods 9 To The Sky utilizes the FTB Questbook, which is an excellent resource for finding recipes, progression tips, and guidance on crafting the powerful ATM Star. Be sure to check it regularly if you’re unsure how to proceed.

My Experience with All the Mods 9 To The Sky

While I found All The Mods 9 To The Sky to be a relatively easy modpack due to the wide variety of mods, it can feel overwhelming at first — which is why I created this guide. Once you start building out your base, I recommend focusing on key steps like using Sophisticated Storage, automating cobblestone generation, and setting up sieving early on. These steps will help you progress faster and unlock access to more complex mods.

In my setup, I built five separate Mystical Agriculture farmland systems, each linked to my Refined Storage network using Modular Routers. This allowed me to efficiently harvest and create essential resources such as iron, gold, diamonds, redstone, and wood using essence. Additionally, I developed an auto-crafting system that upgraded mystical agriculture essence through different tiers. Ultimately, this allowed me to craft Awakened Supremium armor and tools — a major milestone in late-game progression.

For power generation, I achieved a stable energy supply by utilizing Powah's Nitro Magmator (powered by lava) and the Nitro Thermo Generator (powered by boiling water). With this setup, I never had to worry about running out of power. To further expand my energy capacity, I also built a Bigger Reactors system with both a reactor and turbine, supported by enhanced storage via Draconic Evolution.

For mob farming, I constructed a highly efficient mob tower using Mob Grinding Utils. This setup included Dreadful Dirt, fans, an Absorption Hopper, and a Mob Masher combined with fall damage to weaken mobs. Initially, I sorted mob drops using a Sophisticated Storage netherite barrel alongside Create for organization. However, once my storage capacity increased, I streamlined the process by routing items directly into my main storage system via an Ender Chest.
