How to Use JEI (Just Enough Items)

This site showcases the most important recipes in ATM9 To The Sky. For everything else, the JEI mod (Just Enough Items) is the best in-game tool for recipe discovery.

  • Press R while hovering an item to see how to craft it.
  • Press U to see how it's used in other recipes.
  • Search for items in the bottom-right inventory panel.
  • Click arrows or mod icons to filter by mod.
